# Privacy Policy for Holy Bible App

**Last Updated: February 14, 2026**

This Privacy Policy describes how the Holy Bible App ("we", "our", or "us") handles your information.

## 1. Data Collection
The Holy Bible App is designed to be a **privacy-first, offline-only application**. 
- **Personal Data**: We do NOT collect, store, or share any personally identifiable information (PII) such as your name, email address, or phone number.
- **Usage Data**: We do NOT track your usage of the app or collect any analytics data.
- **Local Storage**: Any data you create within the app (such as journal entries or progress) is stored locally on your device and never leaves your device.

## 2. Third-Party Services
We do not use any third-party services that collect your data. 

## 3. Permissions
The app may request the following permissions:
- **Audio/Speech**: Used exclusively for the Text-to-Speech (TTS) feature to read the Bible text aloud.
- **Storage**: Used only to save your local journal entries.

## 4. Children's Privacy
Our App does not collect any data from children or anyone else. It is safe for all ages.

## 5. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you can contact us locally within your community.

---
This app is provided "as is" to help users engage with the Holy Word.
